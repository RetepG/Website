About

Teddy is a black chow chow who came from Missouri and moved to California. He is a professional bodyguard who works at-home and defends the house and gardens from vicious creatures such as birds and squirrels. He is also a foodie who enjoys eating many different things, his favorite being chicken in particular.

Enjoyed activies

Teddy in his free time, enjoys being massaged, especially around his belly area. Although he loves being lazy he is still a dog that needs to be walked which is great because he loves wearing his tiny shoes on adventures.
